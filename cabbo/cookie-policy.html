This Cookie Policy explains how Cabbo ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help us improve your browsing experience and enable certain site functionalities.

Cookies can be:

  • First-party cookies: set directly by us.
  • Third-party cookies: set by external services (e.g., analytics, marketing platforms).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a) Strictly Necessary Cookies

  • Essential for the proper functioning of our website (e.g., saving preferences, ensuring security, enabling forms).
  • These cookies do not require your consent.

b) Performance and Analytics Cookies

  • Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., traffic statistics, most visited pages).
  • We use Google Analytics for this purpose.
  • These cookies are set only with your consent.

c) Marketing and Profiling Cookies

  • Used to deliver personalized communication, such as newsletters and campaigns.
  • We use Mailchimp to manage newsletter subscriptions and related marketing services.
  • These cookies require your explicit consent.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Strictly necessary cookies: processed on the basis of our legitimate interest to ensure site functionality.
  • Analytics and marketing cookies: processed only with your explicit consent.

5. Cookie Management

When you first visit our site, a cookie banner will ask you to accept, reject, or customize your preferences.

You can also manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Manage cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Manage cookies
  • Safari: Manage cookies
  • Microsoft Edge: Manage cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of the site may not function properly.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
